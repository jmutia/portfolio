Ocean Animal

Got a little bit busy at work and thought of brushing up some of the hard skills that I haven't worked on for quite a while.

So I decided to learn basic shapes and lines again using Adobe Illustrator and ended up creating an ocean animal, named Dolly.

Monogram Exploration

I've recently experimented on creating monograms for my wife's mini-business. It's a bit challenging to create something that would probably work as a logo too.

For the typeface, i've chosen Sans Serif with the following fonts: DM Sans, Hind Vadodara, and Noto Sans for experimentation. Gaps are showing differently for each font type, so it's a bit interesting to look at.

After this, the next step is to choose a monogram to explore further and then we'll see how this goes.

3-steps to mastery

I call this poster series, 3-steps to mastery. I feel that this simplifies what each of us needs to understand in order for us to reach our goals. It's a way for us to clear the noise and just focus on the goals that we want to achieve.

And having to set short-term goals doesn't mean we are not aiming for the long term ones, it's just focusing on the path of least resistance. At the same time, it doesn't need to be perfect at all. We all just need to be consistent.
